Garden Services

Our belief is that your garden should do more than just look neat and pretty. It should be low maintenance, healthy, stable and diverse...just like a natural ecosystem.

Garden Maintenance

  • Mowing

    Lawn cutting and edging, removal & compost of clippings, sweeping and general clean up.

  • Pruning

    Mature pruning of larger branches to maintain plant health and safety. Structural pruning of young trees.

  • Fall Clean Up

    Remove deaying leaves with leaf blowing and bagging services / leaf vaccuming and mulching services.

  • Power Washing

    All of our power washing units are 100% self contained and self sufficient to including water.

  • Gutter Cleaning

    Unblocking and cleaning services for removal of leaves, branches, moss and all other debris.
